## Summary

11 general risks registered, spanning correctness (RISK-GENERAL-05), security (RISK-GENERAL-08, RISK-GENERAL-10 — the highest-impact entry), scope discipline (RISK-GENERAL-01), and delivery estimation (RISK-GENERAL-11). RISK-GENERAL-10 (tenant-context loss) is the single highest-priority entry, directly extending ARR-10 and the tenant-isolation risk carried since ADR-0012.
